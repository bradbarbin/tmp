For Consumers

Next Glass eases the anxiety of wine selection by recommending glasses you’ll love and breaking the code of wine menus and endless grocery aisles. With Next Glass, you don’t have to worry about a flowery description from a stuffy server because we make it easy to find a wine that fits perfectly. Discover a new favorite wine and connect with the restaurants and merchants who carry it.

FOR MERCHANTS & RESTAURANTS

Next Glass provides valuable data on the wine consumption habits of your clientele, allowing you to expand your wine offering to reflect what they want, not what a wine distributor tells you to serve. It takes the guesswork out of purchasing and allows you to hone in on the wines that match the DNA, price point and preferences of your customers.

————FOR VINEYARDS————

FOR VINEYARDS

Next Glass offers vineyards the chance to compete on a level playing field. Our scientific process removes the subjectivity from wine and provides distributors, merchants, restaurants and end consumers with a fair, objective, fact-based evaluation of each vintage. Smaller vineyards will gain exposure as their wines rise to the top of the lists, and more established vineyards will find the thrilling challenge of producing the best wines they’ve ever made. Both large and small vineyards will also benefit from the same consumption data as merchants and restaurants, allowing them to target outlets and end users who demand their wine.

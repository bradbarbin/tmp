OUR MISSION

At Next Glass, we have a single mission: to turn the wine industry on its ear by improving and demystifying the wine-buying process.

To do that, we sought out others who wanted to join our wine revolution, fortunately for all of us, they had the knowledge, skills and passion to help make Next Glass a reality.

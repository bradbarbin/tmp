CREATIVE PROBLEM SOLVERS

WANT TO WORK FOR NEXT GLASS?

We can’t blame you; we like to think we’re pretty fun.

Based in Wilmington, North Carolina, we have the beach; some of the best surf on the East Coast; film festivals and street fairs; a movie studio; miles of roads to ride and run, and miles of water to paddle; and did we mention the beach?

In our office we take our work seriously, after all, it’s serious work deposing traditions and removing the fear from wine. We’re all creative problem solving, A-Team workers who thrive on challenges and know how to celebrate our victories. We know our wine, but we know our tools even better. From finance to marketing to SEO to coding, we’re the top talent in town.

Our office is the kind of place you want to work. If you don’t believe it, would the health club convince you? How about catered lunches every week? No? Then check out the fridge full of Red Bulls, iced coffees and all the things to pull you through the day (or night, we work hard). Take time to develop a solution in one of the think tanks, just take a break in the game room or take a walk outside, it’s up to you.

Still not convinced? Then remember one thing: we’re a company that recommends wine, which means we have a bit of it around. Monthly wine allowances allow you to explore the Genome Cellar and discover your favorite bottles, and favorite bosses, along the way.

But that’s just where we are. It’s where we’re headed that’s so exciting. Join our team. Help us get there.
